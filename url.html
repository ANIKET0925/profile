20 Best Students-Friendly School Website Templates 2021

  • July 19, 2021
  • Aigars Silkalns
  • Website Templates
  • 10,676 Comments

With our hand-compiled collection of the best school website templates, you can swiftly establish a site for any educational institution. Recruit more students with a well-thought-out online presence.

Right from students’ admission to exam results can be done on the websites now. It is a must to have a modern website for an educational institution. WordPress education themes give you the advanced option of having a learning management system on your website.

Some of you may not need such advanced features for your site. If you need a site only to showcase your infrastructure, your courses and to get inquiries, HTML school website templates will do the job for you.

HTML website templates have their own pros and cons. Of course, they cannot provide you with as many features as the WordPress themes give you. But it doesn’t mean you shouldn’t use an HTML website templates. Once you installed the HTML school website templates with the contents you want, that’s it you are all set to go.

You no need to think about template updates and other security updates for the HTML templates. If you are going to maintain a passive site in which you are not going to update contents a lot you can choose one of these best school website templates.

Best responsive and easy-to-use school WordPress themes

Divi


It is all possible when you gain access to Divi. Today, you do not need to hire a web developer anymore to build you a website. Regardless of your skill level, even if an utter beginner, with Divi, you will succeed at it any time. This practical solution for all your page ideas is stuffed with amazingness.

Of course, when it comes to crafting school and education pages, you can do those, too, with Divi. There are even several different demos available for you to put them into play right away. With that in mind, you can be confident that the outcome will be ready to go in close to no time.

Divi also follows all the latest trends and practices of the current web. It is mobile-ready, responsive, cross-browser compatible, retina screen friendly, SEO optimized and fast loading. Of course, with Divi, you will also not need to code as it comes with its own integrated builder.

Jevelin


If you would like to welcome on board even more students, a website is an absolute must. With Jevelin, you can now establish one quickly and professionally. Whether you are an experienced coder or a first-timer, with Jevelin, anyone can bring into being the desired page. After all, the majority of work is already done and ready to use. All you need to do is to choose the appropriate demo, fine-tune it and that is basically it. Jevelin also contains WPBakery page builder, unlocking a codeless coding experience. It all happens visually with some dragging and dropping.

Complete creative freedom offers you to alter and modify Jevelin to the very last detail. There are additional forty shortcodes preset for you to integrate them however you fancy. Jevelin is also SEO-ready, fast loading, in tune with all devices, as well as compatible with all popular plugins.

Educlever


Hence the name, Educlever is an educational and school web template with a collection of eight marvelous demos. With Educlever, you can build a page for kindergarten, university, home school, online education and more. The options are there, just put them into play and realize your project in a snap. Have in mind, all the samples are also editable and customizable with a simple technique of dragging and dropping. With that in mind, you need to have no experience with web development and still hammer out a school page with Educlever.

Extra specialties of Educlever contain nine headers, three blog styles, LearnPress LMS plugin, WPBakery page editor, Slider Revolution, sticky header and smooth scrolling. Educlever is a complete solution for your educational project out of the box. Make it yours, brand it and welcome new potential students to join the club.

Kindergartens, universities, colleges, online instructors, well, you get the gist of it, everyone can profit from Pallikoodam.

Powerful and feature-rich plugins and predefined page layouts will make your site not only look great but make it a little breeze to employ and user-friendly as well. You have all the right to modify and improve the appearance of Pallikoodam, so it fits your style best.

MaxCoach comes with an Elementor page builder and an additional forty and counting widgets to work with. The tool is also optimized for SEO and fast loading speed, as well as compatible with all modern web browsers.

Some other unique features of MaxCoach are Zoom video conferences, Duotone icons, Stripe and PayPal payment gateways and quizzes, to name a few. But really, you will find whatever you look for in a school website to sport – no need to use any extensions and whatnot.
